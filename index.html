02The insights-to-action gap

Today, four manual steps
sit between signal and fix.

A human "middleware" engineer manually correlates IoT data with S/4HANA, calls the warehouse for parts, and types a work order. That cycle takes 6 to 48 hours. Roughly 70% of that response time is lost inside manual system lookups — parts availability, work history, and technician scheduling.

05Two-mode autonomy

One loop. Two confidence gates.

Between 85% and 94%, the loop routes to a Maintenance Lead for one-tap confirmation. At 95% and above, Rig-Guard executes — with a 30-minute cancel window.
